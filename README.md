
## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Where are your films made?**
All graphic design, manufacturing, and fulfillment take place from our headquarters in Frederick, Maryland. We serve the entire United States as well as global markets through our international partners.

---

### **Can you make custom wall graphics or wallpaper?**
Yes. Although glass films are our specialty, we also produce custom wall graphics, dry erase finishes, and exterior‑rated film options.

---

### **How tall can you make gradients (custom film)?**
If the width of individual glass panes is **71” or less**, the gradient height can be **indefinite with no seams**.

---

### **I need to match an existing window film — can you match it?**
Usually not a problem.  
Please submit a request through our contact form and include a photo of the film you're trying to match. Our team will assist you through the matching process.

---

### **Am I limited to the finishes on your selection charts?**
Not at all.  
We frequently match **Pantone**, **commercial paint chips**, or **any other color references** you provide.

---

### **What are your lead times?**
Turnaround times vary based on design complexity and project scope.

- **Samples:** typically 3–5 business days  
- **Full jobs:** typically 5–10 business days  

---

### **How much do the films cost?**
Every project is unique.  
Pricing depends on **design time, glass sizes, and overall project scope**.  
Please reach out through our contact form so we can gather the needed details and provide accurate pricing.

---

### **How do I specify Design Studio in my architectural drawings?**
For custom films where details are TBD, add the following to your specification:

> **Custom film by Design Studio — contact info@decorativefilm.com**

For a thorough project specification, contact our sales team. We can generate a unique specification number that we store in our database, ensuring all required details are available when contractors reach out for pricing.
